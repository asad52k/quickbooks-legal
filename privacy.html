Privacy Policy

Iris Finance Assistant

Last updated: March 13, 2026

Overview

Iris Finance Assistant is a personal-use application that connects to the QuickBooks Online API. It is not distributed to or used by any third parties.

Data Collection

This application accesses QuickBooks Online financial data (e.g., profit and loss reports, account balances) solely for the purpose of generating personal financial summaries for its owner.

Data Storage

No financial data is permanently stored by this application. Data is retrieved on demand and used only for the duration of the reporting session.

Data Sharing

This application does not share, sell, or transmit any data to third parties. All data remains under the sole control of the application owner.
